ABOUT CYBERKING TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED

Cyberking Technologies Private Limited was started in 2002 by Mr. Narasimhan who is running the ancestral family stationery business (Est. 1947). We further expanded our horizons as Office Suppliers for all types of Stationery, Papers, Printers, and Computer Consumables.

Our prime customers are leading Private and Government Organizations throughout South India. We are one of the largest commercial partner of Printers as well as cartridges for HP and Canon. Even more, we are authorized dealers for supplying products in Gem Marketplace and have executed multiple orders across India.

Our organization consists of passionate and inspired professionals with the commitment to achieve excellence, customer satisfaction, and provide the best possible solution. We are completing our 20th year with the continued patronage of our valued customers with a sales of INR 35 Crores.
